Mushroom Pizza

Home Menu

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Mushrooms (various kinds, sliced)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Other toppings (onions, peppers, olives, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to the appropriate temperature for your pizza dough.
  2. Roll out or stretch pizza dough to your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  5. Arrange sliced mushrooms (and other toppings, if using) on top.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil.
  7. Sprinkle with minced garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  8. Bake according to your dough instructions until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let cool slightly before slicing and serving.

Optional notes: You can use different types of mushrooms (e.g., cremini, shiitake, portobello). A sprinkle of Parmesan cheese after baking is also delicious.
