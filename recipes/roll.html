Bacon Rolls

Home Menu

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Rolls (e.g., bread rolls, croissants, puff pastry)
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Eggs (optional, for egg wash)
  • Milk or butter (optional, for egg wash)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven (if baking).
  2. Cook bacon until crispy (pan-fried or baked).
  3. Prepare rolls (slice, warm, etc., depending on type).
  4. Assemble rolls: Place cooked bacon inside rolls. Add cheese if desired.
  5. (Optional) Egg wash: Whisk egg with milk or melted butter. Brush on rolls for a golden finish.
  6. (Optional) Bake rolls until golden (if using egg wash or puff pastry).
  7. Serve warm.

Optional notes: You can add other fillings like avocado, tomato, or your favorite sauces. Serve with a side salad.
