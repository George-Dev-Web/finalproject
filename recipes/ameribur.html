Beef Burger

Home Menu

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Season ground beef with salt and pepper. Form patties.
  2. Cook patties to desired doneness (grill, pan-fry, or bake).
  3. Toast burger buns lightly.
  4. Assemble burger: bottom bun, patty, cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles (optional), ketchup, mustard, top bun.

Optional notes: You can add other toppings like bacon, avocado, or your favorite sauces. Serve with fries or a salad.
